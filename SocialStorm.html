SocialStorm shows the liveliest, most happening places in your area right now using a “Doppler like” radar. With SocialStorm you can look around your area for radar activity near you.

It’s even more fun when you’re out of town and don’t know the coolest places around! The check-ins are called "strikes", and just like a Doppler radar, the intensity is color orientated (green/yellow/red).  If there are enough strikes at a single establishment, a “Social Storm” will appear on the radar.

The intensity of the storms use the actual EF scale that tornadoes are scaled with (wind MPH = # of strikes).  When you see a storm you can then tap on it and choose to “CHASE IT” just like a storm chaser! SocialStorm will then automatically open a GPS route to get you there. 

Once you’re actually there, hit the strike button to keep the storm going and level up! The more storms that you've been in, the higher your experience level goes up!
